# Privacy Policy for Pausely

**Effective Date:** April 20, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pausely ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our desktop application. Your privacy is important to us, and Pausely is designed to be a fully offline, local-only application.

## 1. Summary

**Pausely does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** The App runs entirely on your device, stores all data locally, and does not communicate with any remote servers.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

Pausely does **not** collect, store, or process any of the following:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Account credentials or authentication data
- Analytics or usage telemetry
- Device identifiers or hardware information
- Location data
- Contacts, calendar, photos, or other personal files
- Browsing history or activity outside the App
- Advertising identifiers

## 3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Pausely stores the following information locally on your computer, entirely under your control:

- **Reminder settings**: Your chosen intervals for break, water, and screen-time reminders, and whether each reminder is enabled.

This data is saved in a settings file located in your user application data directory (on Windows: `%APPDATA%\pausely\`). This file never leaves your device. Uninstalling the App or deleting this folder removes all stored settings.

## 4. Network Activity

Pausely does not make any network requests as part of its core functionality. The App does not:

- Send usage data, diagnostics, or crash reports to any server
- Download content or updates automatically
- Connect to any cloud service
- Contain any advertising SDKs or trackers

The only time the App interacts with the network is if you click the developer link in the footer ("made with ♥ by Gopu"), which opens your default web browser to an external website. This action is initiated by you, and any subsequent activity is governed by your browser and the destination site's own privacy policy.

## 5. System Permissions

Pausely uses standard operating system features, which may appear as permissions:

- **Notifications**: To display reminder notifications at your scheduled intervals.
- **System tray**: To run quietly in the background so reminders fire while you work.
- **Always-on-top windows**: To show the full-screen break reminder over other applications.

These permissions are used only for the App's stated purpose and do not involve any data collection.

## 6. Third-Party Services

Pausely does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or data-collection services.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Pausely does not collect data from any user, including children. The App is suitable for general audiences.

## 8. Your Rights and Control

Because all data stays on your device, you have complete control over it:

- **Access**: You can view your settings file at any time in your user data directory.
- **Deletion**: Uninstalling Pausely or deleting the settings file removes all data created by the App.
- **Modification**: You can change all settings directly from within the App.

## 9. Security

Your data remains on your device and is subject to your device's own security controls (operating system account, disk encryption, etc.). Because no data is transmitted or stored remotely, there is no risk of a server-side data breach affecting your information.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by a revised "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
